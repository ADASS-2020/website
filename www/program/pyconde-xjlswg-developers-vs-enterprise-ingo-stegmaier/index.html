Developers vs. Enterprise

Community • Use Cases
Talk • PyConDE
Domain Expertise expert • Python Skill Level basic

Ingo Stegmaier

This 30-minute talk will give you an overview about project management, success factors and specialties within enterprises. This will be a guide how you promote internal projects and bring them to success within a highly "political" environment.

Often developers feel misunderstood while managers are overwhelmed with technical details. In this talk I'll present how both world can get a better understanding with each other to collaborate better - and without the pains.

Details: Introduction to project management Success Factors
Be quick but patient
Get your things done and be aware what will happen next Communicate Clear
Go step-by-step Does the Management method matter?
Internal Selling Find a sponsor Define the Stakeholders and tell them what they can expect Not the Code matters, the value matters Distinctive features Stakeholder Management Use Case
